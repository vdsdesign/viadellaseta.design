Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work worldwide?

Yes — we handle export logistics and customs coordination for international projects.

Do you work with private clients?

Primarily with professionals (designers, architects, developers). Private clients are supported on a project basis, ideally with a design/architecture team involved.

Can you manage only part of the list?

Yes — from a single category (e.g., kitchens) to full FF&E.

Do you provide trade pricing?

Yes, for verified professionals and project-based procurement.

Can you support bespoke items and custom finishes?

Yes — we manage factory communication, samples and approvals.

Do you also do smart home?

Yes — Ajax / KNX / Loxone solutions via a partner network, including commissioning.
